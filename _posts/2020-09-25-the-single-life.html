The single and peculiar life is bound
With all the strength and armour of the mind
To keep itself from noyance.

Shakespeare (Hamlet)

Anyone who is a dissident
Undoubtedly would give assent
To this injunction so self-evident,
Even though it lacks a supplement:
That what is done should be done without intent
To bring annoyance to another gent
Or woman!
                      Moreover, context gives one pause
For Thought: a two timing self-server speaks
These lines to benefit the murderous king.
