--- layout: post title: "Hope Eternal" permalink: "hope-eternal" categories: poetry author: John D. Gibson location: Evansville, IN date: 2022-11-12 ---

My loved one's been in exile far too long;
Out of sight, but never out of mind.
And though this next step moves her near,
She's still confined away from me and home.

This parted couple wish the final step
Will quickly come to bring us back to where
        We both should ever be.
