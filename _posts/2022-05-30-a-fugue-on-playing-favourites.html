--- layout: post title: "Mother's Day: a Father's View" permalink: "mothers-day-a-fathers-view" categories: poetry family author: John D. Gibson location: Evansville, IN date: 2022-05-08 ---

To make this day mean more than just
An idle ritual we need to think
  About our son, a man of worth.

Wise in the world's ways his diligence
Brought him success, but best of all he cares
  And shows his love for her who gave
  Him so much care and love.
