--- layout: post title: "Right of Way" permalink: "right-of-way" categories: poetry author: John D. Gibson location: Evansville, IN date: 2021-04-14 ---

1

A single road driver yesterday
Began to nose out on to our highway,
Causing us to swerve and so precipitate
        A three-car pile-up.
That lousy driver would have been OK
In nineteen fifties France where I soon learned,
A few miles from the ferry, traffic entering
        From the right had right of way.

2

Some time ago, in my naivet√©
I thought that wedding Mary would fulfil
My dream of matrimonial bliss, until,
        With barely any delay,
        She found a right of way
        Into my brother's bed.

3

It's twittering times here in the USA.
Where office holders swear an oath to keep
The constitution. Recently, to much dismay
A president has claimed the right of way
        To do as he thinks fit.

Despite attempts to overthrow results
Of an election, steadfast people chose
To assert the democratic way.
