--- layout: post title: "To Love and to Cherish in Sickness and in Health" permalink: "to-love-and-to-cherish-in-sickness-and-in-health" categories: poetry author: John D. Gibson location: Evansville, IN date: 2022-10-20 ---

This forced unwanted separation tests
And strengthens what we promised all those years
Ago. Although we find it hard to bear
Your move to a ward beyond an easy reach
We hope it may restore you to yourself.

Meanwhile we trust the doctors may yet find
An efficacious medication that will
Steer you to a clear and simple path
To tread and I shall gladly take your arm
And stay with you till death us do part.
