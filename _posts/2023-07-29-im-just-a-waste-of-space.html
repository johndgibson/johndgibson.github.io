--- layout: post title: "\"I'M JUST A WASTE OF SPACE\"" permalink: "im-just-a-waste-of-space" categories: poetry author: John D. Gibson location: Evansville, IN date: 2023-07-29 ---

You are not the first or only one
To denigrate yourself, in such a way.
Nor is it necessary to speculate upon
The reason why such deprecations are
Not uncommon.
                        All that your ancient lover needs
To do is reassure you that our love endures
And that he's fortunate to live with such
A kind and loving wife.
